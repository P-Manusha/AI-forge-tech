What is AI?

Artificial Intelligence enables machines to mimic human intelligence—learning, reasoning, and problem-solving.

ML Algorithms: Sets of rules and statistical techniques that help computers learn from data.

What is ML?

Machine Learning is a subset of AI where computers learn from data to make predictions or decisions.

AI Robots: Smart machines that can interact, assist, and even entertain us!

Why AI & ML?

The Evolution of AI & ML

1950s: The Dawn of AI

Alan Turing proposes the Turing Test. The first AI programs are written.

1960s-70s: Early Hopes

Symbolic AI, simple games, and early robots emerge.

1980s: Expert Systems

Rule-based systems dominate industries like medicine and finance.

1990s: Machine Learning Rises

Statistical learning, neural networks, and the internet fuel progress.

2000s: Big Data & Deep Learning

Massive datasets and powerful GPUs enable deep learning breakthroughs.

2010s: AI Everywhere

AI powers smartphones, cars, healthcare, and more.

2020s & Beyond: The Future

Generative AI, autonomous systems, and ethical AI shape our world.

AI & ML Roadmap

  1. Understand the Fundamentals: Math (linear algebra, probability), statistics, logic.
  2. Learn Programming: Python (preferred), R.
  3. Explore Key Libraries: NumPy, Pandas, scikit-learn, TensorFlow, PyTorch.
  4. Data Preprocessing: Cleaning, transforming, visualizing data.
  5. Model Training: Supervised, unsupervised, and reinforcement learning.
  6. Model Evaluation: Metrics, validation, tuning.
  7. Deployment: Bringing models into real-world use.
